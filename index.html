Universal Compliance and Management System

UCAMS is a proprietary governance and compliance framework designed to enforce integrity, authorship, and traceability across digital workflows. The platform simplifies complex compliance processes by enabling fully auditable and traceable digital file control. UCAMS is applicable in fields such as cybersecurity, intellectual property protection, AI governance, and regulatory assurance. The system is currently in an advanced development stage with ongoing enhancements to ensure reliability, scalability, and legal soundness.
