UCAM — Universal Compliance and Management System

Trust enforcement. Protocol governance. Role-bound digital integrity.

What Is UCAM?

UCAM is a sealed protocol designed to verify and enforce digital compliance. It enables cryptographic authorship, traceable process control, and STOP CODE-based governance across files, tools, and teams.

Core Features

  • Role-specific operational modes (Engineer, Analyst, Auditor)
  • STOP CODES that prevent unauthorized changes
  • Hash-based file sealing and .sig signing
  • UCSM metadata and trust_level tracking
  • Internal charter and protocol enforcement

Where UCAM Applies

  • Cybersecurity workflows
  • Authorship & IP validation
  • AI model compliance
  • Developer documentation sealing
  • Medical, legal, and technical audit trails

Access Policy

UCAM is a closed protocol system under structured development. No collaborators, partners, or open access is currently available. The system is governed and maintained by its original author.
